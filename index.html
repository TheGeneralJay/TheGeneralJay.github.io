About Me

Hello! My name is Amanda Gurney, a student currently attending George Brown College in Toronto, Ontario. I am in my sixth and final semester in the Computer Programming and Analysis advanced diploma program. I am currently a SAP (System Analysis Program Development), Inventory, and Pricing specialist at Morello's Independent Grocer in Peterborough, ON. With my colleagues, we aid this branch of Loblaws by keeping the store inventory up to date and adjusting pricing or electronic label issues as they come. I also find myself doubling as technical support and often become the line of communication between our IT help desk and our in-store employees.

With this experience at work, I recognized that I wanted to dig deeper into the world of programming and software in general. I applied for this program through George Brown College and started off with very little background in any meaningful programming languages. Throughout my time learning, I have applied new topics to assignments as they arise and have dedicated myself to being a lifelong learner -- as that is what we must be in any rapidly advancing field. I am available for contact easily through my email or phone number, each listed above on this page. If you are interested in working with me on a project, or just want to discuss more, please feel free to get in touch!
